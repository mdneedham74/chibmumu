% Abstract -----------------------------------------------
\begin{abstract}
  \noindent
 An emulator is developed that allows to reproduce the mass resolution
seen for $\chic$ Dalitz decays in the full simulation. The emulator
performance is validated with range of similar decay modes and is
concluded to reproduce the full simulation results with an uncertainty
of $3 \%$. The insights gained from this study have wider
applicability. 
\end{abstract}
